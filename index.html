ABOUT MY FISH

I love Pets, Especially Fish.

Love is always love; we don't know how it comes about. For me, it comes from my fascination with fish. I began to develop this passion when I was just 10 years old. At that time, I knew very little about it, but I found myself immersed in breeding and grooming fish, even selling them to my classmates. After some time, I took a break from this hobby, but now I've reignited my journey with fish as my beloved pastime.
Today, I have a diverse collection of fish, including guppies, mollies, fighters, and various other species. I raise and care for these fish in my home, and I also sell them both online and in my local community.

My Logo



My Fish Journey.

My journey began with betta fish, also known as fighter fish. Initially, I started with just two pairs of fighter fish. During the first three breeding attempts, I had no knowledge of fry food, and unfortunately, I couldn't raise a single fry. However, I soon discovered live cultures and other essential aspects of fry care. With the help of these foods and newfound knowledge, I managed to successfully raise 50 to 100 fries in each batch. This achievement boosted my self-confidence as a hobbyist.
Now, I've expanded my breeding efforts to include guppies and other fish species' fry, which has given me even more motivation to continue my journey of fish breeding and grooming.
